Our Mission

Our mission is simple: simplify pet pawrenthood. Our lives are already busy and often hectic. Let PET-ME help you manage your pet's health. With PET-ME, you will be able to update your pet's health records and it will automatically set a reminder for when you should schedule your pet's next vet appointment. You can also learn about what the medication that was administered would help prevent and what kind of possible side-effects to be in the look out for.
