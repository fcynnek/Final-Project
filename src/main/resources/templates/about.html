About Us

The founder of PET-ME is a volunteer at the NYC's Animal Care Centers where he started as a dog walker, adoption councelor, and outreach advocate. In 2020, when COVID-19 affected opperations at the shelter, volunteers where not allowed to be in the premises to assist. He then started fostering and that was how he ended up adopting his pitbull dog, Miss Frizzle (named after the cartoon "The Magic School Bus").

Once the pandemic was slightly under control and vaccines where more widely available, Kenny wanted to be closer to the community and assist in the areas most affected. He then switched roles to be an animal handler for the shelter's mobile vaccination clinics. An animal handler is the person who restrains the animal while the veterinary doctor administers the vaccines to the pet. This is to ensure that the animal does not attach nor bite any personnel during the vaccination.

Surprisingly, he noticed that many of the pets would miss their annual (or bi-annual in some cases) vaccines. *Et voila* Why not create something that will help pet pawrents track and remind them of their pet's vaccines and medications? I mean... Us humans have Apple Health. So why not create the "Apple Health" equivalent for pets?
